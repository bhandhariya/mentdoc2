Scientific Diagnosis & Digital Prescription

MentDoc has been designed to aid MHPs in accurately diagnosing the mental illness which a patient is suffering from, basis the symptoms which the patient exhibits. using ICD 10 classification of mental and behavioural disorders. Doctors can also prescribe medication digitally using this platform, which eliminates the need for paper usage. This makes MentDoc environment friendly.

Effective communication and Seamless collaboration

The e-prescription provided by the doctor will be immediately accessible to the partner pharmacy enabling seamless communication and collaboration amongst the doctors, patients and the partner pharmacies thus making the whole process easier and quicker. The doctors also have the option of partnering with pathological laboratories which shall know of the tests to be administered as soon as the prescription is generated

Intelligent follow- ups and reminders

Leveraging the features of MentDoc, mental health professionals can schedule appointments on future dates and can also book time slots during which they would like the patients to consult them. The appointments can be cancelled and rescheduled as per the convenience and availability of the mental health professional. The MHPs also get reminders prior to an appointment being underway.

Patient Engagement and Education

MentDoc is a free patient care platform that offers patient care education and engagement customization at the point of care and on the go. Mental health practitioners can utilize this application to educate patients about disorders, treatments and precautions to observe to prevent the onset of mental illness
