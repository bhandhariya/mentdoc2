Reliable & Secure Data

To ensure data security, MentDoc makes use of Amazon's cloud based Web Server, which is 256 bit encrypted. The data protection measures are compliant with HIPAA & DISHA standards.

Simplified and Streamlined Operations

MentDoc offers Seamless Practice Management to MHPs who can make use of MentDoc in order to access their patients' records across multiple establishments where they consult patients and operate out of, like clinics, hospitals, residences, etc.

MentDoc is solely meant for MHPs

MentDoc has been designed exclusively for the mental health domain. It is meant for Mental health professionals like psychiatrists, psychologists, psychoanalysts, psychotherapists & mental health counselors.

Digital Documents generated are as per the MHA,2017

MentDoc generates documents such as reports, summaries & prescriptions in accordance with the regulations laid down by the Mental Healthcare Act, which was amended in 2017. This implies that patients struggling with mental illnesses can now access their medical records digitally.

One Stop, Comprehensive Solution

MentDoc is an all-inclusive data & practice management solution which caters to the needs of all registered mental health professionals.

Scientific Diagnosis & Digital Prescription

MentDoc has been designed to aid MHPs in accurately diagnosing the mental illness basis symptom(s) being exhibited by the patient(s), using ICD 10 classification of mental and behavioral disorders. MHPs can also prescribe medication digitally using MentDoc.
